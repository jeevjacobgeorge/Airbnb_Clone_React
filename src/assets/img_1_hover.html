What you'll do

We will meet online and we will have a unique approach to learning the symbols in your birth chart maps and understanding yourself and your significant ones a little better.

I will bring insights into your Numerological profile, Human Design, and Psychological, Traditional, and Vedic Astrology.

We will look at the structure of your natal chart and I will explain how to understand your birth chart in simple easy-to-learn steps, and how it relates to your life patterns and the inherent energies you can tap into.

In the end, you will receive an email with a PDF of your natal map.

For groups there are discounts available, Please PM contact me for info regarding group prices **

Team building, astrology workshop for teams. Suitable for TEAM BUILDING groups and CORPORATE EVENTS ( booked by teams from Airbnb, Meta, Microsoft, Netflix, Spotify, Linkedin, Grammarly, Hubspot, Shopify, Wix, Scribd, Yelp)

* This is an interactive experience great for a PRIVATE session but also wonderful for FAMILY members, love couples, group events, groups of FRIENDS, birthday celebrations, GIFTS, weddings, and bachelor parties.

* The experience is designed for 1 to 30 guests

Please keep in mind for groups bigger than 10 guests, the experience can extend to approximately 90min.
